## 1. The Services
Foovii provides a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that enables the Merchant’s customers to view digital menus, interact with 3D/AR product visualisations, and place orders via QR code scan (“Services”). Where the Merchant subscribes to a plan that includes payment processing, all customer payments are processed through the Merchant’s own Stripe account (a “Stripe Connected Account”). Foovii is not a party to any payment transaction between the Merchant and its customers. The Services are provided on an “as-is” basis, as further described in Clause 10.

2.3 **IP Ownership:** Foovii retains all intellectual property rights in the 3D models, software, and platform. You are granted a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence to access and use the Services and associated content solely for your internal business purposes during the term of this Agreement.

3.2 **ACL Compliance:** You must comply with the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) and all applicable food safety laws. You are solely responsible for the quality, safety, and accuracy of the food and beverages provided.

## 4. Fees and Payment

**Current Plan Details are set out in Schedule A.**

4.1 **Subscription:** You agree to pay the subscription fee specified in Schedule A in accordance with the payment frequency and due date specified in Schedule A. ## 4A. Payment Processing (Menu+Pay Plan Only)
This clause applies only where the Merchant subscribes to a plan that includes payment processing.

4A.1 **Merchant’s Stripe Account:** Payment processing is performed exclusively through the Merchant’s own Stripe Connected Account. The Merchant is solely responsible for establishing, maintaining, and complying with the terms of its Stripe account. Foovii acts solely as a technology platform and is not a payment service provider.
4A.2 **Application Fee:** Foovii will collect an application fee (as specified in Schedule A) from each customer payment transaction via Stripe Connect. This fee is automatically deducted before the remaining funds are settled to the Merchant’s Stripe account.
4A.3 **Stripe Fees:** All payment processing fees charged by Stripe (including but not limited to card processing fees, currency conversion fees, and any other Stripe charges) are passed on to the customer at the point of payment. The Merchant's prices are received in full, net of Foovii's application fee.
4A.4 **Chargebacks and Disputes:** The Merchant is solely responsible for all chargebacks, payment disputes, and fraud-related claims arising from customer payment transactions processed through the Merchant’s Stripe account. Foovii has no liability for any such chargebacks, disputes, or claims.
4A.5 **Refunds:** All refund decisions and processing are the sole responsibility of the Merchant through the Merchant’s Stripe account. Foovii’s application fee on refunded transactions is non-refundable unless otherwise agreed in writing.

9.1 **Term:** This Agreement commences on the Commencement Date and continues on a month-to-month basis until terminated.
